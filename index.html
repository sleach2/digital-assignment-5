Arena

  • High Concept: An endless waves of enemies spawn player kills to get more points
  • Player Experience Goals: I just want them to have fun.
  • Genre: This game is without a doubt a shooter.
  • Game play: Use arrow keys to move about. Move the pointer over targets and hold the left mouse button to fire.
  • Prototype goal: I am testing projectiles and messing with timers.
  • Features: I want to try to get it set up so that if you kill all of the enemies before the time limit of a wave is over the remaining time is added to the number of enemies that will be spawned next wave.
  • Setting/Premise: An underground arena.
  • Story: You are solider that has been captured by the enemy and is now being forced to fight for your freedom. The only problem is that the enemy has no intention of letting you go
  • Target Audience: Anybody really.
  • Play time: However long it takes for player to die.
  • Strategies: What player strategies do you expect will be effective at playing this game?
  • Prototype Assets: Pahser examples, games other people have made, and stuff I came up with.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: There is more than likely a game like this I just don't know if there is or isn't.
